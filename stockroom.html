Rolling Inventory System

1. Beginning Inventory

Start of day inventory from yesterday ending.

2. Stock In

Items received from supplier delivery.

3. Stock Out

Used items from POS, Kitchen & Bar.

4. Waste

Expired, damaged or spoiled items.

5. Ending Inventory

Actual remaining stock at end of day.

6. Auto Carry Over

Ending today becomes beginning tomorrow.
